Signs You Need an Oil Change

Don't wait until your engine is damaged. Visit our Nashville shop if you notice:

  • Oil change light or check engine light on your dashboard
  • Dark, dirty oil on the dipstick (should be amber)
  • Engine running louder than normal
  • Oil smell inside the cabin
  • Exhaust smoke that's darker than usual
  • It's been 3,000-7,500 miles since your last oil change
  • Low oil level on the dipstick

Conventional vs. Synthetic Oil: Which Do You Need?

Conventional oil is refined from crude oil and works well for older vehicles and standard driving conditions. It's more affordable but requires more frequent changes (every 3,000-5,000 miles).

Synthetic oil is engineered for superior performance. It flows better in cold weather, resists breakdown in hot temperatures, and lasts longer (5,000-7,500+ miles). Many newer vehicles require synthetic oil - check your owner's manual.

Not sure which is right for your vehicle? Our Nashville mechanics will check your manufacturer's recommendations and help you choose the best option for your car and budget.

Frequently Asked Questions - Oil Change Nashville

How much does an oil change cost in Nashville?

Conventional oil changes at Majesty Auto Repair start around $35-$50. Synthetic oil changes typically cost $65-$90, depending on your vehicle's requirements. We use quality oil and filters to protect your engine.

How often should I change my oil?

Most modern vehicles with synthetic oil can go 5,000-7,500 miles between oil changes. Older vehicles or those using conventional oil should change every 3,000-5,000 miles. Check your owner's manual or ask our Nashville mechanics for your specific vehicle.

Should I use synthetic or conventional oil?

Synthetic oil offers better protection, especially in extreme temperatures and for longer intervals. Many newer vehicles require synthetic. Conventional oil works fine for older vehicles with regular driving habits. Our mechanics can recommend the best option for your car.

How long does an oil change take?

Most oil changes at Majesty Auto Repair take about 20-30 minutes. We also perform a basic inspection of your vehicle while we're under the hood, checking fluids, belts, and filters.

What happens if I don't change my oil?

Old oil breaks down and loses its ability to lubricate your engine. This leads to increased friction, overheating, and eventually serious engine damage. Regular oil changes are the most important maintenance for your vehicle's longevity.

Do you check other things during an oil change?

Yes! During every oil change at our Nashville shop, we check your air filter, cabin filter, fluid levels, belts, and perform a visual inspection. We'll let you know if we spot any issues that need attention.
