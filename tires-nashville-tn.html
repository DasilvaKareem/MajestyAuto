Signs You Need Tire Service

Don't wait until you're stranded. Visit our Nashville tire shop if you notice:

  • Low tread depth - Use the penny test (if you can see Lincoln's head, time for new tires)
  • Uneven tire wear - May indicate alignment or suspension issues
  • Cracks or bulges in the sidewall
  • Vibration while driving (could need balancing)
  • Flat tire or slow leak
  • TPMS warning light on your dashboard
  • Tires older than 6 years - Rubber degrades over time

Frequently Asked Questions - Tires Nashville

How much does tire repair cost in Nashville?

Flat tire repair at Majesty Auto Repair typically costs $20-$40 if the damage is repairable. New tire prices vary based on size and brand, but we offer competitive pricing and can help you find affordable options for your vehicle.

Can you repair a flat tire or does it need to be replaced?

It depends on the location and size of the damage. Punctures in the tread area can usually be repaired. Sidewall damage or large punctures typically require replacement. Bring your flat tire to our Nashville shop for an honest assessment.

How often should I rotate my tires?

We recommend rotating your tires every 5,000-7,500 miles or with every other oil change. Regular rotation promotes even wear and extends tire life, saving you money in the long run.

How do I know if I need new tires?

Check for tread depth below 2/32 inch (the penny test), uneven wear, cracks, bulges, or vibration while driving. If your tires are more than 6 years old, have them inspected even if they look fine. Our Nashville mechanics can check them for free.

Do you offer wheel alignment in Nashville?

Yes! Majesty Auto Repair offers wheel alignment services to ensure your tires wear evenly and your vehicle handles properly. We recommend an alignment check when you install new tires or if your car pulls to one side.

What tire brands do you carry?

We work with multiple tire distributors to offer a range of brands at different price points. Whether you need budget-friendly tires or premium brands, we can get you what you need at competitive Nashville prices.
